Structure & safeguards

Who issues it, who manages it, where it is listed

Every instrument on the platform sits inside a regulated chain. This is that chain, drawn out: the issuer, the portfolio manager, the post-trade agents and the venue — so the regulatory architecture reads as an asset, not as a footnote.

Exchange traded instruments

A listed security, a segregated strategy

HERAKLIT, Tech Megatrend Tactical, LC Invest — Value Edge and IVN Global are issued by iMaps ETI AG and bought like any other listed security, through the investor's own bank or broker.

Certificates & notes

Issued through a protected cell

Noctiluca Capital and Zalphyx Yield Strategies are issued through protected cells on the GenTwo securitisation platform — each cell ring-fenced from the others, with post-trade functions handled by regulated agents.

Funds

Managed under the AIFMD

The nine funds in the range are alternative investment funds — six Italian reserved AIFs and three Malta-domiciled Notified AIFs — with Framont & Partners Management Ltd as the appointed management company and AIFM.
